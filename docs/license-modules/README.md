# License Modules / Módulos de Licença

## 📜 Overview / Visão Geral

**English**: These modules extend the base GPL-3.0 license of Magisk_Rafaelia with additional ethical, social, and philosophical frameworks that guide the project's development and use.

**Português**: Estes módulos estendem a licença base GPL-3.0 do Magisk_Rafaelia com frameworks éticos, sociais e filosóficos adicionais que guiam o desenvolvimento e uso do projeto.

---

## 📚 Modules / Módulos

### [Module 1: Semantic-Legal / Módulo 1: Semântico-Legal](01-SEMANTIC-LEGAL.md)

**English**: Establishes legal and ethical framework, aligning with international legislation (UN Declaration of Human Rights, UNICEF Convention, LGPD/GDPR), AI ethics standards, and dual licensing model (social/inclusive GPL-3.0 + commercial).

**Português**: Estabelece enquadramento legal e ético, alinhando com legislações internacionais (Declaração Universal dos Direitos Humanos, Convenção UNICEF, LGPD/GDPR), normas de ética em IA e modelo de licenciamento dual (GPL-3.0 social/inclusiva + comercial).

**Key Topics**:
- International human rights legislation
- Data protection (LGPD/GDPR)
- AI and ethical computing standards
- Dual licensing framework
- Responsible disclosure and ethical hacking
- Non-maleficence clause

### [Module 2: Rapport and Relations / Módulo 2: Rapport e Relações](02-RAPPORT-RELATIONS.md)

**English**: Strategies for ethical communication, accessibility, and social inclusion, promoting a collaborative and respectful environment with multilingual support and diverse community engagement.

**Português**: Estratégias de comunicação ética, acessibilidade e inclusão social, promovendo um ambiente colaborativo e respeitoso com suporte multilíngue e engajamento comunitário diverso.

**Key Topics**:
- Ethical communication principles (transparency, active listening, inclusive language)
- Digital accessibility (WCAG 2.1, assistive technologies)
- Multilingual support
- Social inclusion and diversity
- Community code of conduct
- Rapport building strategies
- Social responsibility

---

### [Module 3: Paradoxes and Hypotheses / Módulo 3: Paradoxos e Hipóteses](03-PARADOXES-HYPOTHESES.md)

**English**: Explores paradoxes in AI, security, computing, and spirituality through scientific and philosophical analysis, proposing testable hypotheses and understanding frameworks.

**Português**: Explora paradoxos presentes em IA, segurança, computação e espiritualidade através de análise científica e filosófica, propondo hipóteses testáveis e frameworks de compreensão.

**Key Topics**:
- AI paradoxes (explainability vs. performance, alignment, singularity)
- Security paradoxes (perfect security, disclosed vulnerabilities, root access)
- Philosophical paradoxes (free will, digital consciousness, meaning in computing)
- Mathematical and logical paradoxes (Gödel incompleteness, self-reference)
- Testable hypotheses and experimental frameworks
- Continuous paradox analysis

### [Module 4: Union of Efforts / Módulo 4: União de Esforços](04-UNION-OF-EFFORTS.md)

**English**: Vision of cooperation among developers, users, and social entities, aligned with universal human rights and sustainable development goals (SDGs), establishing governance models and shared resources.

**Português**: Visão de cooperação entre desenvolvedores, usuários e entidades sociais, alinhada aos direitos humanos universais e objetivos de desenvolvimento sustentável (ODS), estabelecendo modelos de governança e recursos compartilhados.

**Key Topics**:
- Stakeholder roles and contributions (developers, users, researchers, NGOs, companies)
- Multi-level cooperation model
- Alignment with UN Universal Declaration of Human Rights
- Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs 4, 8, 9, 10, 16, 17)
- Governance models (rough consensus, technical veto, rotation)
- Shared resources (knowledge, code, infrastructure, support)
- Ethical financing mechanisms
- Long-term vision (2030, 2035, legacy)

### [Module 5: Ethics and Truth / Módulo 5: Ética e Verdade](05-ETHICS-TRUTH.md)

**English**: Alignment with RAFAELIA principles to ensure integrity, transparency, and protection of life. Establishes the sacred cycle (EMPTY → ACTION → FULL → FEEDBACK) and fundamental ethical principles.

**Português**: Alinhamento com princípios RAFAELIA para garantir integridade, transparência e proteção à vida. Estabelece o ciclo sagrado (VAZIO → VERBO → CHEIO → RETRO) e princípios éticos fundamentais.

**Key Topics**:
- RAFAELIA philosophy (sacred cycle: EMPTY → ACTION → FULL → FEEDBACK)
- Fundamental ethical principles (truth, non-maleficence, beneficence, justice, autonomy, accountability)
- Protection of life (prohibited vs. encouraged use cases)
- System integrity (multi-layer verification)
- Algorithmic transparency (three-level explainability)
- Commitment to scientific truth
- Continuous ethical evolution
- RAFAELIA Oath

## 🔗 Integration with Base License / Integração com Licença Base

**English**: These modules are considered **annexes** to the main GPL-3.0 license. While the GPL-3.0 provides the legal framework for software distribution and modification, these modules establish:

1. **Ethical guidelines** for development and use
2. **Social commitments** to the community
3. **Philosophical foundations** for decision-making
4. **Governance principles** for the project
5. **Protection clauses** for human rights and life

**Legal precedence**:
- GPL-3.0 terms take legal precedence for licensing matters
- These modules guide ethical and social conduct
- In case of conflict, prioritize: Life > Ethics > Law > Convenience

**Português**: Estes módulos são considerados **anexos** à licença principal GPL-3.0. Enquanto a GPL-3.0 fornece o enquadramento legal para distribuição e modificação de software, estes módulos estabelecem:

1. **Diretrizes éticas** para desenvolvimento e uso
2. **Compromissos sociais** com a comunidade
3. **Fundações filosóficas** para tomada de decisão
4. **Princípios de governança** para o projeto
5. **Cláusulas de proteção** para direitos humanos e vida

**Precedência legal**:
- Termos GPL-3.0 têm precedência legal para questões de licenciamento
- Estes módulos guiam conduta ética e social
- Em caso de conflito, priorizar: Vida > Ética > Lei > Conveniência
