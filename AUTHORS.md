## Historical Timeline

### Superuser/Root Management History
- **2008**: Zinx Verituse - Original su concepts
- **2010**: Adam Shanks - SuperUser application
- **2015**: Pierre-Hugues Husson - phh's Superuser
- **2017**: John Wu - Magisk initial release
- **2017-2025**: John Wu - Continuous Magisk development
- **2025**: Rafael Melo Reis - RAFAELIA Framework addition

### Magisk Evolution
- **v1.x** (2017): Initial systemless root implementation
- **v2.x** (2017): MagiskSU and module system
- **v20.x** (2019-2020): Zygisk introduction
- **v24.x-v28.x** (2021-2025): Modern Magisk with enhanced features
- **RAFAELIA** (2025): Ethical computing and governance framework

## Acknowledgments

### Original Magisk
Special thanks to **John Wu** (@topjohnwu) for creating and maintaining Magisk, one of the most important tools in the Android modification community. His work has enabled millions of users to customize and enhance their Android devices.

### Historical Foundation
Thanks to the pioneers of Android root management: Zinx Verituse, Adam Shanks (ChainsDD), and Pierre-Hugues Husson, whose early work laid the foundation for modern Android root solutions.

The RAFAELIA Framework brings ethical computing principles and governance to the Magisk ecosystem, building upon the strong technical foundation with a focus on transparency, accountability, and responsible technology use.

Thanks to the entire Android modification community, ROM developers, module creators, and users who have supported and contributed to the ecosystem.
